Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Focuses on basic literacy, social-emotional skills, introducing letters, numbers, shapes, and collaborative play-based learning.

Elementary School

Develops core skills in reading, writing, math, science, and social studies, emphasizing problem-solving, creativity, and cooperative learning activities.

Middle (10-16) Years

Expands to advanced content like algebra, literature analysis, and lab sciences, while fostering independence, critical thinking, and collaboration skills.
